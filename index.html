Why volunteer?

There are plenty of reasons to volunteer at Slush in 2015. The event draws together a crowd of 15.000 tech entrepreneurs, VCs and journalists to Helsinki, Finland on November 11-12, 2015.

Volunteers will be helping out in various tasks during the event week. We have about 30 different tasks where volunteers can work, so on there is definitely something for everyone. You will meet interesting people, learn a lot of new things and get a chance to see the how one of the biggest startup and tech events in the world is made - from the inside. Volunteer spots are in high demand and new volunteers are chosen continuously – so apply as soon as possible. The application period ends on 10.09.2015 and every applicant will be informed of the outcome by 24.09.2015 the latest.

If you have any questions concerning volunteering at Slush 2015, please send them to volunteer@slush.org

Volunteer application

Fill out the form below and apply to volunteer at Slush 2015.

The Volunteer Kick Off event will be on 8th October, which is the first event for all Slush volunteers. Before Slush volunteers will have couple training sessions, info lecture and excursion to Messukeskus. During the event volunteers will be working in their tasks and enjoying the event. After that the volunteers will have the legendary afterparty.

In addition to an amazing experience each volunteer will receive a Slush t-shirt & work certificate from Slush!
